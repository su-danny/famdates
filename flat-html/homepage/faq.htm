What is famdates?

- famdates is the ultimate portal connecting sports, fitness, and nutrition enthusiasts globally, allowing for an interactive and informational forum encompassing their common interests and passions.

What Is FanZone?

- The Fan Zone feed is where sports fans all across the world come together to talk sports, connect with other fans online or at local events and pubs. Fan Zone gives you live score updates for all your favorite teams.

What is Get Fit?

- The Get Fit feed is where users can come together to discuss workouts, post the latest workout tips and videos, discuss supplements and nutrition, find trainers, gyms, dietitians all in your local area and world wide.

What is Game Time?

- Game Time is the feed on the site where active people can find other like-minded people who share similar interests to network with. This feed allows users to find local pick up games, discover leagues and coaches for their teams or simply explore local events such as marathons and tournaments.

Who can join famdates?

- famdates is open to all users 13 years of age and older, free of charge.

What do I need to join?

A valid email address, or facebook account. Information including images on your Sports Freak profile are available to all other users. Please be sure to use good judgment when posting information and/or images on your profile.

Does famdates sell my personal information?

- Rest assured, famdates will never sell any of your personal information to any 3rd party and/or company. In some instances, famdates will use such information to improve our services within our secure platform in order to save you time, while improving your experience

What is the star rating all about?

- The star rating represents how active you are on the platform determined by number of posts, followers, and how popular your posts become by way of your engaging content.

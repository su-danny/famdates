Sports Freak is a social networking web app that revolutionizes the way people connect and engage all within one online portal that revolves around sports, fitness and nutrition. At Sports Freak, we believe fans of all levels can learn, interact and live out their love of the game by connecting with fellow sports and fitness minded individuals. Take your active lifestyle to the next level: create your personal profile, connect with people on and offline, and discover the resources to live, breathe and love the life of a Sports Freak!


The roots of Sports Freak began in July 2012 in the heart of Silicon Valley, the birthplace of such game-changers as Google and Facebook. CEO and Founder Vahe Tashjian, was looking to fill a need to relate to fans sharing the same passions, who were dedicated to an active, healthy lifestyle. He searched online for a social networking solution and couldn’t find any platforms that delivered what he was seeking. Hence, the Sports Freak was born!
